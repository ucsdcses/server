FAQs

What is BlueprintCS?

How many times have you written code reminiscent of spaghetti? I've done so more than once. BlueprintCS aims to combat italian dishes and bad code design. Unlike a regular hackathon, this is a designathon, a day dedicated to software spec and architecture. Think of it as a 10 hour hackathon to see who can design the best blueprint for their software project. You can work in teams of up to 4 people and prizes will be awarded to the best blueprints! Free meals, snacks, and drinks are provided throughout the event!

What if I don’t have a team?

At the start of the event, we will have a team forming session to ensure you’ll be able to find teammates. If you wish to work alone, that is also an option! We allow up to four people per team. If you have a team, make sure each person applies and includes all other members’ names in their applications.

Who can attend?

BlueprintCS is open to all UCSD students 18 years of age or older!

What if I don’t have experience?

We will have workshops throughout the event to teach you how to design the foundations of Grade A software!

What should I bring?

Make sure you bring your student ID, computer, chargers, etc.

Sounds great! Do I have to pay?

Nope! The food, swag, and an unforgettable experience are on our tab!

What is the deadline to apply?

Applications are due on May 6th at 11:59pm. You will hear back from us shortly after the deadline!

What if I have an unanswered question?

We're always willing to answer any additional questions! Feel free to email us at csesucsd@gmail.com!
