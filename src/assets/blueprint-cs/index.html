The Low Down

How many times have you written code reminescent of spaghetti? I've done so more than once.

BlueprintCS aims to combat italian dishes and bad code design. Unlike a regular hackathon, this is a designathon, a day dedicated to software spec and architecture. It's why this site has so many pictures of the UCSD CSE Building.


[ We're here to help you forget about *p = *((int**)(((int*)(((int)(*p))^1))+1)); ]

Experience

Don't Got It:
Learn with your peers in company-sponsored workshops and challenges as you work to design the foundations of Grade A* software.

Got It:
Team up and jump straight into the competition to create the most well-designed design for a software project, show off in front of your friends and strangers, and get some schweet prizes.


*Not as in the algorithm, as in the footnote. (Actual grades will not be assigned.)
