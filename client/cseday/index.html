A Day for UCSD CSE

CSE Day is an event dedicated to the passion and education of all who are interested in CS or CE, and highlights the broadest of opportunities, be it in industry or academia. CSE Day serves an important purpose in the computing community on campus, by showing students what important things they can do in the industry before and after they graduate, helping to demystify the pathways they can take to become successful, and showing them the opportunities that their peers, the faculty and the attending companies can offer them.
